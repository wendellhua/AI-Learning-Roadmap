## 💡 Core Learning Principles | 核心学习原则

### The AI-Native Learning Formula | AI原生学习公式

```
Traditional Learning | 传统学习:
  Read → Memorize → Practice → Test
  阅读 → 记忆 → 练习 → 测试

AI-Native Learning | AI原生学习:
  Question → AI Dialogue → Validate → Apply → Iterate
  提问 → AI对话 → 验证 → 应用 → 迭代
```

### Key Mindset Shifts | 关键思维转变

1. **From Memorizing to Understanding | 从记忆到理解**
   - ❌ Old: Store information in your brain | 把信息存在脑子里
   - ✅ New: Know how to find and validate with AI | 知道如何用AI查找和验证

2. **From Solo to Collaboration | 从单打独斗到协作**
   - ❌ Old: Figure everything out yourself | 自己搞清楚一切
   - ✅ New: AI is your thinking partner | AI是你的思考伙伴

3. **From Passive to Active | 从被动到主动**
   - ❌ Old: Wait for teachers to teach | 等老师来教
   - ✅ New: Proactively explore with AI guidance | AI引导下主动探索

## 🎯 Quick Start Guide | 快速入门指南

### Step 1: Choose Your Focus | 选择你的重点
Based on your role and needs, pick 1-2 areas to focus on first.
根据你的角色和需求，先选择1-2个领域重点学习。

### Step 2: Set Up Your Tools | 配置你的工具
- Free tier: ChatGPT, Claude (basic)
- Paid tier: Cursor, Copilot, Claude Pro

### Step 3: Practice Daily | 每日练习
- Use AI for at least one task every day
- Reflect on what worked and what didn't
- Iterate your prompts and approaches

### Step 4: Build Your Prompt Library | 建立你的提示词库
- Save effective prompts
- Categorize by use case
- Share and learn from others
